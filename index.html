Instructions

Goal: Be the first to get three pairs of cards, where each pair totals 10.
Ace is taken as 1. In the case of 10, J, Q and K, you have to get a pair of that card, for example, two Jacks make a pair. Examples of pairs include: A and 9, 7 and 3, K and K, 10 and 10.
How to proceed: You start off with 6 cards. Discard a card to start the game. At each turn, you either take the top card from the discard pile, or draw a card from the well. If you do not have 3 pairs of cards, you will have to discard a card to end your turn. Both you and the computer will do the same until either of you ends up with 3 pairs of cards.
Tip: Try not to discard a card you suspect the computer wants.
